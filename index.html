ABOUT

Who is Jaque?

I've worked in the Business Administration industry for almost 17y. I always loved my job and aimed to keep growing and find new challenges. But after all those years, I just felt like meh - it looked like I had hit the ceiling, and I knew I yearned to learn more, something different.

So, I decided to seek a career change, and Software Engineer seems to be a good path. I was always surrounded by tech people and always enjoyed their talks and being part of solving the problems they faced.

The enrollment at Software Engineer Immersive Bootcamp at GA was the beginning of this new journey. I've been working towards building a solid career as a full-stack developer. I'm passionate about solving problems and delivering quality. Writing clean and accessible human code matters to me. I'm happiest when I'm creating, learning, exploring and thinking about how to make things better and in a simple way.
